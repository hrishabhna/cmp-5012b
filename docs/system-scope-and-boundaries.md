# System  Responsibilities

- Tracking the user's health (in-scope)
- Producing weekly data on the user's health (in-scope)
- Allowing users to create their own profiles (in-scope)
- Tracking what the user eats (in-scope)
- Allowing users to update their profile (in-scope)
- Giving the user recommendations on what exercises to do in order to improve their health based on the weekly health reports (out-of-scope)
- Giving the user recommendations on what food to eat in order to improve their health based on the weekly health reports (out-of-scope)
- Allowing users to add each other as friends (out-of-scope)
- Giving the user recommendations on what exercises their friends do in order to improve their health based on the weekly health reports (out-of-scope)
- Giving the user recommendations on what food their friends eat in order to improve their health based on the weekly health reports (out-of-scope)
- Sending the user reminders to send their health data (exercise performed, food eaten etc) into the system (out-of-scope)

# Existences outside the system (users, external devices, organisations or other systems)

- Gyms (may want to intergrate health tracker into them)
- Mobile phones (could create a dedicated app for health tracker)
- People exercising (could use health tracker to make their workout more efficient)
- Insurance company (may want to integrate  health tracker with insurance products if it's successful)
- People dieting (could use health tracker to better manage diet)
- Chefs (could use health trackers to create heal)
