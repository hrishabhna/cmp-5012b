# Personas

## Persona 1: Janice Smith 
- Age:45
- Background: Janice is a full time worker as an event coordinator and as a result of this she usually orders takeout as it is more convenient and easy to purchase. 
- Goals: She wants to be able to be able to switch to meal preped food as well as adding more whole foods and increase protien and fruit intake. Furthermore, she would like to be able to workout 4 days a week and be able to record the days that she has worked out and set future goals for herself so that she can see how much of an impact the change has made. 
- Frustrations: One of the main fustrations that she has is that meal preping is time-consuming and finding the time is hard as she her work usually tends to be a schedule that changes every day. This makes it difficult for her to prep as well as finding the time to exercise.  
- Typical scenario of use: She will be able to add the meals that she has had during the day as well as long the days that she has completed an workout session and this way she will be able to keep log of the impact and see how it has improved her lifestyle. In addition to this, she would be able to decrease on the consumption of fast food as well.
